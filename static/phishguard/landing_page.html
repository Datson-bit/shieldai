The Two-Step Attack Pattern

Fraudsters rarely hack databases anymore. Instead, they exploit the human element. They impersonate trusted agencies to get inside wallets.

"My phone buzzed with a message that GTBank was blocking my account. I clicked. I typed my OTP. In less than 1 minute, the savings I raised for my daughter's school fees were gone."

Judges and security officers must realize the speed and emotional urgency of these threats. ShieldAI intercepts at step one (recognizing the threat link) and invalidates step two (monitoring biometric access profiles).
